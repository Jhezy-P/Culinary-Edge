🍲 Step-by-Step Recipes

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
Instructions:
  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Heat butter in pan.
  3. Pour eggs and stir gently.
  4. Cook until creamy, season, serve.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic & Onion
  • Olive oil, Salt, Pepper
Instructions:
  1. Boil spaghetti.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil.
  3. Add beef, cook thoroughly.
  4. Add tomato sauce, simmer 15 min.
  5. Serve over pasta.

Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorns
Instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce and garlic.
  2. Add vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns.
  3. Simmer 30–40 minutes.
  4. Reduce sauce until slightly thick.
  5. Serve with rice.

Pancakes

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions:
  1. Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat pan and pour batter.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side.

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:
  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & Pepper
Instructions:
  1. Preheat grill.
  2. Brush salmon with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Grill until flaky and cooked through.
